About Daffodil Software Ltd

Daffodil Software Ltd. is a leading custom software services company based in Bangladesh, with a dedicated Development and Support Office in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. Since our establishment in 2001, we have been delivering tailored software solutions and comprehensive system integration services for a wide range of industries.

Our team of experienced professionals brings deep expertise in software development, hardware management, and modern digital technologies.

With a strong foundation built on trust, innovation, and technical excellence, Daffodil Software Ltd. continues to empower businesses by providing scalable, efficient, and cutting-edge digital solutions. Our multi-skilled teams have successfully implemented projects using a variety of platforms, programming languages, and development tools, enabling us to meet the dynamic needs of our clients across Bangladesh and global markets.
